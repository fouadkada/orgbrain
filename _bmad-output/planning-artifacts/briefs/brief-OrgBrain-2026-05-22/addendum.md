# OrgBrain Brief — Addendum

_Depth that earned a place but belongs downstream — in a PRD, architecture doc, or investor deck — rather than in the brief itself._

---

## Success Criteria

For reference when moving to PRD or when pitching to a metrics-driven audience:

- A new hire reaches working productivity in under 4 weeks (vs. 3–6 month industry baseline)
- Users ask OrgBrain questions they previously would have sent to a colleague — and get accurate, sourced answers
- At least one executive-layer signal (bus factor, departure risk, or alignment gap) is acted on within the first 90 days of deployment
- Net revenue retention above 120% — organizations expand seats as OrgBrain proves value

---

## V1 Out-of-Scope (Parked for V2+)

Explicitly deferred — not abandoned:

- Full executive intelligence suite: shadow org chart, decision velocity report, strategy-execution gap, departure risk signal
- Board-level institutional memory report
- Portable professional memory (employee-owned knowledge graph)
- Email ingestion (privacy complexity warrants a separate phase)
- Deep compliance certifications: SOC 2 planned post-launch; HIPAA/FedRAMP post-PMF

---

## Open Questions

Questions that surfaced during the brief session that will need answers before or during PRD:

1. **Pricing model:** Per seat, per org, or tiered by feature layer (individual / team / executive)? SMB-first suggests flat org pricing with a usage or seat cap.
2. **Privacy boundary:** What is the policy for DMs vs. public channels? Will OrgBrain ingest private Slack channels by default, or only public ones? This is likely a configurable admin setting — but the default matters.
3. **Hallucination / accuracy floor:** What confidence threshold does OrgBrain need to reach before surfacing an answer vs. saying "I don't have reliable information on this"? This is a product quality bar, not a feature.
4. **Who is the anti-champion?** The person at the company whose influence is built on information asymmetry will resist OrgBrain actively. How does the sales motion handle or route around them?
